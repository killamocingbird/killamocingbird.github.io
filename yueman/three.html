I used to tell my sister all the time about my wife. She's going to be raised in China but moved to America during high school. She's going to want to have girls, and have an endless thirst for knowledge. She's going to have long black hair, be skinny, and shorter than me. These are some of the endless lists of demands I used to present late at night to my sister. I don't even remember them all. She would laugh and tell me, "As you meet more people, your list will change and get shorter." I used to think, "How dare she? I think I know myself and my tastes very well!" The truth is, you are quite close to that list -- but of course there are places where you don't meet the insane standards I set when I was in high school. However, when I called you the other day, I really did mean what I said. I think that if I were to talk to my sister now, I'd say that she was right. My list did change. And it's all your fault.
